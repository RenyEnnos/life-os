# Product Guidelines

## Design Philosophy ("Deep Glass")
-   **Visuals:** Premium SaaS aesthetic with strategic glassmorphism (overlays only), deep rich dark mode (`#0a0a0a`), and fluid, purposeful animations.
-   **Layout:** Three-zone dashboard (Now, Today, Context) adapting to time of day.
-   **Typography:** Clean, modern, high-contrast.
-   **Offline-First:** Explicit offline indicators and optimistic UI updates everywhere.

## User Experience Metrics
-   **Instant Interactions:** <50ms for UI feedback.
-   **Data Operations:** <200ms latency.
-   **AI Latency:** <2s for complex analysis, <500ms for quick suggestions.
